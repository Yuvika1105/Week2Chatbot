HR Leave Policy

Overview:
Employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per calendar year. Leave is accrued pro-rata from
the employee's start date and is scheduled through the HR self-service portal.

Entitlement and Accrual:
- Full-time employees: 20 days per year, prorated for part-time staff.
- Public holidays are in addition to annual leave.
- New hires begin accruing leave from their first day; entitlement for the first year is calculated pro-rata.

Carryover and Expiry:
- Up to 5 unused days may be carried into the following year with manager approval.
- Any carried-over days must be used within the first 90 days of the new year or they will expire.

Types of Leave:
- Annual Leave: Planned time off for vacations and personal rest.
- Sick Leave: Employees are entitled to 10 days of paid sick leave per year; long-term sickness requires a
	medical certificate and coordination with HR.
- Emergency / Compassionate Leave: Short-term paid leave for bereavement or family emergencies; contact HR.
- Parental Leave: Governed by local legislation; contact HR for company-specific top-ups and eligibility.

Requesting Leave:
- Submit leave requests at least 2 weeks in advance for planned time off via the HR portal.
- For urgent or last-minute leave, notify your manager and HR as soon as possible.

Unpaid Leave:
- Unpaid leave may be granted at the discretion of the manager and HR; terms will be agreed in writing.

Contact:
For questions about leave balances, exceptions, or special cases, contact the HR team through the HR portal or
email hr@company.example.
