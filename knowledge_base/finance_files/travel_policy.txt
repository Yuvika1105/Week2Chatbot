ACME Corporate Travel Policy

Booking and Approvals:
- Employees booking travel must use the corporate travel portal.
- All business travel must be approved by the department manager before booking.

Air Travel:
- Economy class must be used for all domestic flights and flights under 6 hours.
- Business class is permitted for international flights exceeding 6 hours with VP approval.

Meals and Accommodations:
- Hotel bookings are capped at $200 per night for standard business destinations.
- Daily meal expenses are reimbursed up to $75 per day.
- Alcoholic beverages are not reimbursable.
