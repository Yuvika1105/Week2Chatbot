Public FAQ

Welcome to the company public FAQ. This page contains quick answers to common employee questions.

General:
- Office hours: Monday–Friday, 9:00–17:30 local time.
- Company holiday schedule is published on the intranet's calendar.

Getting Help:
- For IT support, submit a ticket via the IT service portal or email it-support@company.example.
- For HR inquiries (payroll, benefits, leave), check the HR portal or email hr@company.example.

Onboarding and Policies:
- New employees should review the onboarding checklist and complete mandatory security and compliance training
	during their first week.
- Company policies, employee handbook, and code of conduct are available on the intranet.

Where to Find Documents:
- Corporate templates, expense forms, and travel guides are in the Policies & Forms section of the intranet.

Other Questions:
If you can't find what you're looking for, contact the internal help desk or ask your manager for direction.
